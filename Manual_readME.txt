STORY:
WildBeast is a 3D escape stage-level game.
You need to find the portal which save you from the monster in every stage.
You have your own ability to shoot some bullets to the monster and make it push away.

CONTROL:
 	W:To move front
	S:To move back
	A:To rotate to the left side
	D:To rotate to the right side
	Shift:To sprint
	Mouse Left Click:To shoot bullets
	R:To reload you bullets

Hint:
Always reload your bullets
